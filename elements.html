Programming is an Art

The Art of Programming.

I got into programming because I had a passion and desire to learn. Everyday the world is changing and the technology around us. It is this need to learn that has fueled and continues to inspire me. It has always fascinated me that the world is code. I feel a need to provide more value and grow my skills to contribute to the community; thus growing myself both individually and spirtually. I know it may sound silly to some, yet technology is an essential component in my happiness. Having a computer in your pocket and not using it for valuable growth as an indivual; is a dirt road to depression. I would much rather use technology to my advantage; On a highway to happiness.
